How it works

ShelfSpy uses a generative-AI powered workflow system that monitors your worksite. It notifies you of hazards or unproductive environment. It enables you to dynamically monitor every aspect of your worksite, including inventory, obstructions, and damage; and allows you update the system in real-time.

Safety First

Protect your team and assets with real-time hazard detection. ShelfSpy continuously monitors your warehouse for:

  • Fallen boxes that could cause accidents
  • Damaged shelving that compromises stability
  • Any other safety risks that could lead to disruptions or injuries
